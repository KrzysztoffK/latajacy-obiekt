Santa Simulator 1999

Instructions

Your objective is to collect as many gifts as you can and avoid the demons that will try to get in your way.

Evert gift is worth one point.

Randomly, there will spawn an extra gift worth 10 points.


Randomly, there will spawn a follower that will follow you for 5 seconds.

You lose if he manages to catch you.

Good luck!
